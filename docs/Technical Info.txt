File Format Information - Updated WED MAY 10 2019

CRT(Creature) 

Simple Description of Human Creature body types:

	Hum = Human

	F & M = Female and Male, respectively

	Fat, Nor, Str, & Wir = Fat, Normal, Strong & Wiry respectively

Van Buren then breaks these down into parts.

	<Race><Gender><Bodytype>

This combination loaded by the tech demo will then load the apropriate G3D(Granny 3d) model.

These may be used by the Tech Demo's script commands:

GetGender()
SetGender()
GetEthnicity()
SetEthnicity()
GetSubrace()
SetSubrace()
